This is my first blog. I had the idea to start a blog for a couple of months, but never got to building it. During the end of summer, I stumbled upon a George Hotz's twitch archive channel and saw a couple of clips, which really fascinated me and I started to research him. That is when I found his blog. I decided that I would like my blog to look similarly, so I checked what he used to make it. Turns out it was Jekyll - a static site generator which used ruby. There were 2 problems with it: 
1. I had never heard of Jekyll before.
2. I had never used ruby before.

These 2 factors combined for a pretty long blog development time, since my attempts to install Jekyll resulted in multiple errors. Then school started, so I decided to leave it for a bit. 2 months pasted and I stilled hadn't installed Jekyll, mostly because I refused to watch YouTube tutorials because I knew that I could not always rely on there to be good YouTube tutorials, so I was determined to learn to read the documentation. At least that was until yesterday when I gave in and searched for a YouTube tutorial. And here I am, after a 20-minute YouTube tutorial I had successfully setup Jekyll. Maybe I should have given up sooner.

Moving on, since this is the first blog, I don't really have what to write about, but I would like to start continuously writing on here. I think there should be what to write about. I mean I am preparing for an entrance exam to a couple of universities, so at the least I could write about my progress in that.

I am probably going to spend around 10 minutes today setting up this blog.

Anyway, have a nice day.
